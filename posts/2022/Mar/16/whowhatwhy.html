03/17/2022

> Is the blinking cursor mightier than the sword? _

The internet, the mother of modern movements, equalizes the distance information must travel from any one mind to reach any other.

In the early stages, there was the online wing of New Atheism, and until recently, we were leaving religion in droves. In 2020, you found a new favorite portmanteau, and it was either plandemic or covidiot. Her denizens, through you and a sea of others, are given an abundance of chances either to kill or to keep every time ameteur medical advice is solicited. Recently as of writing this, Russia has invaded Ukraine, and she has assumed the role of an actor in the conflict- a double agent.

With the right information placed in the right location at the right time on these wires, it is possible to bend the flow of global currents. Whether it's for the worse or the better all epends on what is actually true. The situation calls for skeptics, which is perfect, because I seldom believe anything anymore. I will be the first to admit I am a bit lost.

-Abbott Laust
